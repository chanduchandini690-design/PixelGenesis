Veri-Crypt

Emphasizes trust, verification, and blockchain flavor.

About the Project

Traditional identity systems store credentials on centralized servers, risking privacy loss. Veri-Crypt uses blockchain-based DIDs and Verifiable Credentials to give users full control of their digital identity.

Key Features

Self-Sovereign

Users fully own and control their identity credentials.

Secure & Private

Encrypted credentials with blockchain-level security.

Verifiable Credentials

Share credentials safely without exposing full data.

Interoperable

Works seamlessly across platforms.
